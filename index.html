What are the Camps?:

The KTP Coding Camp is a fun and short program designed to help CU students strengthen their coding skills and collaborate on real-world projects. Students can engage in hands-on workshops, learning new programming techniques and problem-solving strategies while building connections with peers passionate about tech. This camp is a great opportunity for both beginners and advanced coders to grow and develop in a supportive environment!

Why Learn to Code?:

Learning to code unlocks a world of possibilities. It boosts your problem-solving skills, opens doors to exciting careers, and empowers you to create amazing things – from websites to games and beyond. So why not give it a shot?

C++:

About Us:

We’re currently pledge members at Kappa Theta Pi, and are driven by the belief that technology can make a positive impact on the world. Our goal is to help our peers grow not just as a coder, but as a person who uses their skills to do good. Through collaboration, mentorship, and community, we strive to build a future where technology is used for meaningful and lasting change.
